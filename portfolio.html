Bubble Skincare Wireframe: Developing the Marketing Plan

For this class project, I designed a website wireframe that captures the playful spirit of Bubble Skincare. It's not just a website, it's an interactive story! Visitors embark on a journey where they are the hero, facing common skincare challenges. Bubble Skincare, with its fun and effective products, acts as their guide

Highlight: showcased the power of storytelling in user experience. By personalizing the experience and highlighting solutions to real problems, this wireframe compels visitors to see Bubble Skincare as their partner in achieving radiant skin.
